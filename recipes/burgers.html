Shrimp Burgers


Description

This recipe will list the ingrideints for the shrimp burgerss.

It is a simple, yet effective and delicious recipe for all.


Ingridients

  • ¾ pound raw, peeled shrimp
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ¼ cup bread crumbs
  • 1 small lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons frozen butter cut in small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil, or as needed

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Roughly chop 3 or 4 shrimp and place in a bowl. Pulse remaining shrimp in a food processor and transfer to the bowl with the chopped shrimp.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine egg, bread crumbs, lemon juice, lemon zest, seafood seasoning, and black pepper in a bowl. Add shrimp and butter. Form mixture into 3 patties and refrigerate at least 30 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook patties until browned, 4 to 5 minutes. Flip and cook until second side is browned, 4 to 5 minutes more.
